What This Run Does

  1. Stage 1 — FRV Scoring
    For every account, compute:
    FRV = (Balance × Recovery Probability) − Legal Cost
    Recovery probability is derived from credit score, employment status, asset ownership, prior payment, and debt age. Accounts where FRV ≥ the threshold above pass Stage 1.
  2. Stage 2 — Exclusion Filtering
    Accounts are checked against all mandatory exclusions: active attorney placement, bankruptcy, statute of limitations expiry, disputed status, military / SCRA protection, deceased debtor, and debt below $500. Any single exclusion disqualifies the account.
  3. Output — Legal Eligible List
    Accounts that pass both stages are marked Legal Eligible and shown on the results list, ready for attorney placement or further action.
