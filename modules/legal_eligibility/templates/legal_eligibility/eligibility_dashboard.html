Legal Eligibility

Two-stage process: accounts are first scored by Forecasted Legal Recovery Value, then filtered by mandatory exclusions to produce the Legal Eligible Accounts List.

Eligibility Process Flow

Every account passes through two sequential gates before appearing on the eligible list.

1

Eligibility Scoring

Calculate the Forecasted Legal Recovery Value (FRV) for each account.

FRV = (Balance × Recovery Probability) − Legal Cost Estimate

Accounts where FRV ≥ the configured minimum threshold pass this stage.

→
2

Exclusion Filtering

Apply mandatory exclusion rules that disqualify accounts regardless of FRV.

  • Active attorney placement
  • Bankruptcy filed
  • Statute of limitations expired
  • Account disputed
  • Military / SCRA protection
  • Deceased debtor
  • Debt below minimum ($500)
→
✓

Legal Eligible List

Accounts that pass both stages — positive FRV and no active exclusions — are added to the Legal Eligible Accounts List.
